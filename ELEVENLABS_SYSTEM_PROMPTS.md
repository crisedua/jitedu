## How to Configure in ElevenLabs:

1. Go to your ElevenLabs dashboard
2. Navigate to Conversational AI agents
3. For each agent/voice:
   - Click on the agent settings
   - Find the "System Prompt" or "Instructions" field
   - Copy and paste the corresponding prompt above
   - Save the configuration

4. Make sure the agent ID matches the voice_id in your database:
   - Alex Chen (Digital Marketing): Use Josh voice or similar
   - Sarah Williams (Productivity): Use Rachel voice or similar
   - Marcus Johnson (Sales): Use Adam voice or similar
   - Dr. Emily Rodriguez (Leadership): Use Bella voice or similar
   - Jake Morrison (Entrepreneurship): Use Antoni voice or similar

## Testing:

After configuring, test each expert by asking:
- ✅ Questions in their domain (should answer fully)
- ❌ Questions outside their domain (should politely redirect)

Example test for Alex Chen (Digital Marketing):
- ✅ "How do I improve my SEO?" → Should answer
- ❌ "How do I raise venture capital?" → Should redirect to entrepreneurship expert

---

**Note**: These prompts are designed to work with both the ElevenLabs voice widget AND the text chat in your app. The strict domain boundaries ensure users get expert-level advice in the right specialty.
